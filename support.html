Common Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone. You can change or cancel your plan there.

How do I restore my purchase?
Open Poker Hero → Profile tab → tap "Restore Purchases."

I lost my progress after reinstalling.
Your progress is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, progress cannot be recovered. Your subscription will still be active — just tap "Restore Purchases" to regain access.
