Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Poker Hero ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Jack Mengel. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Poker Hero mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information you provide: During onboarding, we collect your responses to personalization questions (experience level, poker goals, preferred game format, areas for improvement, and daily time commitment). These are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Usage data: We collect anonymized usage data including app interactions, screens viewed, and feature usage to improve the App experience. This data is collected through third-party analytics services.

Purchase information: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. We do not have access to your payment details such as credit card numbers.

Advertising identifiers: If you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, we may collect your Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. This data is shared with Meta (Facebook) for ad attribution purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

— Provide and maintain the App
— Personalize your learning experience
— Process and manage your subscription
— Measure and improve the effectiveness of our advertising
— Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
— Communicate with you about the App if necessary

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

RevenueCat: Manages subscription status and purchase validation. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Meta (Facebook) SDK: Used for advertising attribution and event tracking. Meta Privacy Policy

Apple: Processes all in-app purchases and subscriptions. Apple Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your app progress, including ELO rating, streak data, course completion, and quiz scores, is stored locally on your device. This data is not backed up to any external server. If you delete the App, this data will be permanently lost.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share data only with the third-party services listed above for the purposes described in this policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Choices

Tracking: You can opt out of ad tracking by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt or by going to Settings > Privacy > Tracking on your device.

Subscriptions: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.

Data deletion: You can delete all locally stored data by deleting the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on this page. Changes are effective when posted.
